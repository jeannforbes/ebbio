First Look

A new player spawns in as a little organism. This player will see other particles around them. They move their mouse, the little player-orb follows it.

The player eats a parasite and gets a little bigger. That orb vanishes. The player sees another player, bigger than them.

That other player bites the intrepid newbie, who shrinks and has little particles come out of their body.

Confused, the intrepid newbie pushes buttons to try and fight back, but is unable to harm their aggressor and dies.

The newbie spawns in again. This time, nobody attacks them and they grow more.

When they gain enough mass, the newbie is given a choice of weapons to have after evolving.

Remembering how effective it was when they were bitten, the newbie decides to evolve a powerful bitin' jaw.

Now prowling the world, the less-fresh newbie finds themself some fresh meat and devours them whole, growing and evolving all the while.

Steam vents populate the world, spewing particles of delicousness

Description

Ebbio is a mouse-controlled online multiplayer game.

The goal is to be the largest thing in the world for as long as you can.

Players are represented by organisms that follow the mouse.

Upgrades add little organs to the player's body.

Players consume crumbs on the map to increase in mass.

At certain Growth Thresholds, players become temporarily immortal and can choose between a pair of skills- some active, some passive.

At the third Growth Threshold, players choose to become a Symbiote or Parasite.

Symbiotes

Symbiotes are cooperative players that focus on growing in teams inside the bodies of bigger Symbiotes.

The bodies of large Symbiotes contain hotspots, which spawn green Symbiote Crumbs.

When entering large Symbiotes, small Symbiotes play a defense game around the Symbiote's hotspots, protecting them from Parasites.

Symbiotes are immune to the attacks of other Symbiotes, and when damaged only produce Parasite Crumbs.

Symbiote expansion is represented by a cluster of smaller organs growing around their main body.

Parasites

Parasites are singular players that focus on eating Symbiotes and other Parasites.

Parasites are snake-like creatures, smaller than Symbiotes but denser and unable to be entered.

Parasites enter the bodies of large Symbiotes, attacking the hotspots. Destroyed hotspots drop a large amount of red Parasite Crumbs.

Parasites can damage all other players, and produce Parasite crumbs when damaged.

Parasite expansion is represented by new organs being added to their body.

Mechanics

The first Growth Threshold is at 20 mass, the second at 50. After that, the Growth Threshold doubles every time.

Before becoming a parasite or symbiote, new Growth Thresholds are represented by additional organs on the player's body.

  • Choosing Teeth on Growth Threshold 1 will cause your organism to sprout a little jaw.
  • Choosing spikes will cause a long horn to sprout out the front of your organism.
  • On Growth Threshold 2, choosing the shield will cause a flexible, dark shield to appear on the left side of the player's organism.
  • Choosing Dive will add little fins to the back of a player's organism.
